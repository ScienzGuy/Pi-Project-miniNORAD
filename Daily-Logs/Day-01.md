## 🚀 Professional Context: The "So What?"
**Why this matters for Infrastructure:**
Understanding the ML Lifecycle (MLLC) is critical for controlling cloud spend. By distinguishing between **Supervised Learning** and **GenAI**, I can architect solutions that use the most cost-effective tool for the job—avoiding "over-engineering" a problem with an expensive LLM when a simple **Amazon SageMaker** classification model would achieve higher precision at 1/10th the cost.

**Key Verbs for my Portfolio:**
- **Mapped** business requirements to AWS AI Managed Services.
- **Analyzed** data preparation workflows to mitigate model bias early in the pipeline.
